# SOFI Investment Thesis (Work in Progress)

## Business Overview
SoFi is a financial technology company that offers services like personal loans, student loan refinancing, banking, and investing through its digital platform.

## Why I picked this company
I chose SoFi because it is a growing fintech company and I wanted to understand how newer financial platforms compare to traditional banks and the overall market.

## Key Observations
- The stock shows higher volatility compared to the S&P 500
- Price movements seem more sensitive to market sentiment
- Growth potential exists but comes with higher risk

## Risks
- High volatility
- Dependence on interest rates and macro conditions
- Competition from both banks and other fintech companies

## My Take
Right now, I see SoFi as a higher-risk, growth-oriented company. It may offer upside, but it is less stable than the broader market. I need to analyze it more before forming a stronger opinion.
